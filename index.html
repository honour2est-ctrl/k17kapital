Who We Are

A West African–Focused Enterprise, Headquartered in Dubai

K17 Kapital L.L.C is a diversified enterprise with its corporate base at the Dubai International Financial Centre (DIFC). We operate at the intersection of Oil and Gas, Shipping, Logistics, Dredging, and Aviation.

Our business model is carefully structured around the clients we are privileged to serve — multinational corporations, government institutions, private enterprises, and strategic partners who rely on efficiency, reliability, and excellence in execution.

Through superior retail and bulk marketing, we are active buyers and sellers of premium petroleum products, ensuring consistent supply and value across our operational markets.

What We Offer

Integrated Solutions Across Key Sectors

From petroleum product transport to offshore accommodation, our diversified fleet and operational capabilities allow us to serve complex, multi-sector needs across West Africa and beyond.

01 🛢️

Product Tankers

Safe and efficient transportation of refined petroleum products to various markets across West Africa via our product tanker fleet.

02 ⛽

Gas Distribution

MT Arcturus3 — an LPG tanker with a 1,700 MT storage capacity for Liquefied Gas Product distribution across the region.

03 💧

Water Services

K17 No.3 — a 1,000 MT stainless steel fresh water bunker ship, supplying maritime and industrial water needs.

04 🌾

Agriculture

A full-spectrum agribusiness firm involved in permaculture planning, production processing, sales, marketing, and distribution.

05 ⚓

Dredging

Maintaining safe and navigable waterways for ports and harbors with specialized dredging equipment that removes sediment buildup.

06 🚛

Diesel Services

Reliable diesel fuel supply supporting the fuel needs of maritime, industrial, and logistical operations at scale.

07 🚂

Rail Terminal Services

Operator of Rail Oil Terminal involved in the transportation of Diesel, PMS, and other refined petroleum products.

08 🚢

Platform Supply & Security

Transporting essential equipment and deploying security vessels to protect offshore operations and supply chains.

09 🛫

Aviation

Comprehensive aviation support for business and operational needs, including fuel logistics and air charter coordination.

10 🏗️

Upstream Oil Operations

Comfortable offshore accommodation for oil workers, plus essential upstream support services for oil field operations.
